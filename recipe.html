How to Prepare Guyanese Chicken Curry

Description

This dish is sure to awaken your tastebuds with a rich spicy flavor

Ingredients

  • Cubed potatoes
  • Quartered chicken legs
  • Garlic
  • Scallion
  • Thyme
  • Curry seasoning
  • Water

Directions

  1. Season chicken garlic, scallion and thyme, allow to sit in refridgerator for at least 4 hours
  2. Simmer curry seasoning in saucepan for 4-5 minutes
  3. Add seasoned chicken to saucepan, and cook slowly for 15 to 20 minutes
  4. Add cubed potatoes and enough water to cover the chicken
  5. Bring to a boil and then turn the fire down to low and simmer until the potatoes are tender
  6. Remove from heat and add chopped scallion on top
